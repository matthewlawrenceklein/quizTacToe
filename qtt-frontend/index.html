Quiz Tac Toe - Rules
The goal of Quiz Tac Toe is to achieve three 'X's in a row, in any direction. This is accomplished by correctly answering trivia questions. Incorrect answers will add 'O's to the board. Try to win in as few moves as possible, and watch out for increasingly difficult questions!
